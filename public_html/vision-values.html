Our Vision

LAFA envisages a society of restored and transformed people with sustainable livelihood, by providing education and welfare support to the most vulnerable children and young people in the communities.

To see young people in Africa educated, empowered and living free from poverty, abuse and exploitation.

Our Mission

Provide a platform for strategic private and public sector collaboration, aimed at promoting sustainable socio-economic development activities to transform aid and philanthropy to accelerate community-led change.

To attract volunteers and partners who are passionate in helping children achieve their dreams and full potential.

Our Core Values

At LAFA, we live by four Core Values that are at the center of our office culture and drives our daily work.

Our Belief as Chistians

We believe in the deity and humanity of Christ, in His virgin birth, in His sinless life, in His miracles, in His vicarious and atoning death through His shed blood, in His bodily resurrection, in His ascension to the right hand of the Father, in His present rule as Head of the Church and in His personal return in power and glory.

We believe in the power of great ideas and that these ideas can come from anyone, anywhere, at any time.

We Value the Less Fortunate

Together we seek to facilitate an engagement between the poor and the affluent and share a quest for justice, peace, reconciliation and healing in a broken world.

Our vision for every child is life in all its fullness. Our prayer for every heart is the will to make it so.

By taking the time to evaluate the needs of each community and individual, we're better able to help them achieve a brighter future.

We are Accountable

We are committed to holding ourselves accountable for the promises we make to our supporters, partners, staff, and most of all, children. This means looking back at our commitments, celebrating our successes and learning from any mistakes in order to do more and do better.

We are focused on expanding our mission. We believe in being fully transparent to ensure we are making the largest impact.

We Listen, Act, Learn & Repeat.

We continually experiment. We fail quickly and productively. We use data and feedback to guide our course.

While maintaining a firm belief of unity in the Body of Christ, we partner freely with the poor, donors, other organizations and churches of all faiths to work together and make a collaborative effort for justice.

These Core Values drive our long-term strategies and short-term goals, and they underpin our mission to inspire breakthroughs in the way the world treats children and achieve immediate and lasting impact in their lives.

Our Value

Every child, everywhere has the right to a happy and healthy life and your donation goes a long way to helping create a better life for them.
