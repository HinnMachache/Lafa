About Us

Loveline Aid For All - LAFA Foundation is a registered non-profit organization working to address critical issues facing children, Widows and vulnerable communities, through meaningful volunteer service programs. We work with volunteers to provide and improve the quality of education, healthcare, nutrition and care for vulnerable children and widows while working in partnership with rural villages to create and establish sustainable solutions for community growth.


Our Christian faith leads us to believe every person is created in God’s image and deserves life in all its fullness. By showing our experience of the unconditional love of Jesus Christ in our work. We believe that through hope, restoration and renewal is possible even in the most difficult places, which is why we work through relief, development and advocacy approaches in contexts where the most vulnerable children struggle to survive.

Our Promise!

As LAFA support team, we are committed to advancing diversity, equity and inclusion in our workplace as well as in the communities we serve through sustainable initiatives that drives social change and improves the lives of underprivileged people by producesing a profound impact on health and welfare.

Some of the step we take to ensure that Children & Adults are safeguarded:

  • Make children, their families, communities and our staff aware of the standards of behaviour they can expect from us and of how they can raise a concern

  • Ensure all representatives are aware of the risks of child and adult abuse, their safeguarding responsibilities, and the requirement to report.

  • Embed 'safer programming' steps throughout our programme development and implementation.

  • Complete criminal records checks and referencing for all staff during recruitment.

Sustainability approach

  • 01

    Food

    Every child deserves the food and with proper nutrition early in life, children can grow, develop and thrive to their full potential.

  • 02

    Education

    With the help of corporate partners, we focus on improving the access to quality of learning opportunities for all children.

  • 03

    Water & Health

    Empowering community stakeholders and leaders to identify, plan and promote improvements in health and nutrition practices and access to clean water, sanitation and hygiene to provide children and their families a chance to flourish.

  • 04

    Transformational empowerment

    Promoting economic advancement through community empowerment so that they may have access to opportunities, services, resources and gain improved decision-making in economic activities, resulting in improved wellbeing. We also promotes equitable systems through which communities can benefit from equitable policies, laws and institutional practices.
