Mobile Food Pantry-Migori Village, Kenya

Help children protect their futures from hunger!

Hunger knows no borders, no boundaries and no limits. It's a force that drains a child's energy, silences their play, and turns their dreams to dust. They should be exploring with their friends or expanding their minds in class. Instead, too many are worrying about when their next meal will be.

With the help of local partners; we can deliver lifesaving nutrition programming and long-term livelihood support on a truly global scale.

 
 

Our Free food access program.

There are many programs to help children and teens have food before, during and after school. We have the resources to assist children in bringing nutritious meals and more home, whenever thereis a need.

  • Backpack Program

    Backpack food programs offer families free groceries every friday to take home and get them through weekends. Food backpacks include healthy, easy-to-prepare and fresh produce.

  • In-School Pantries

    This is a service introduced within school level that is self-serve to all students. community partners, and school administrators to provide free food for students and their families.

  • Pop Up Pantries

    Pop Up Pantries allow us to come into a community with a box truck full of food; including, pantry staples, fresh produce and dairy.
