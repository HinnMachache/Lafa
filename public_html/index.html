Who We Are

Love-Line Aid For All

Love-Line Aid For All (LAFA) is a non-profit organisation that helps orphans, widows and vulnerable children in Africa by providing access to quality education, health and their day to day basic needs.


We offer regular, consistent support by delivering sustainable, long-term solutions to those children in our care who are in need and able to develop over time, giving them the chance to create a brighter future. We LAFA believes that every child, everywhere has the right to a happy and healthy life.

Our Goal!

To empower a child and their community for a better future

Our goal is to empower a vulnerable or an orphan child in the their community to live a full and productive life through the provision of education, shelter, and medical care in a more caring and loving environment.

Maximize Impact

LAFA family combines your monthly donations with those of other sponsors of that community to make the biggest impact on the worst problems children face.

Community Led

We work hand-in-hand with the community to fix the root causes of poverty – so every child can grow into who God created them to be

Child Focused

We ensure children are empowered to develop healthy minds, bodies, and spirits, so they can achieve long-term success for themselves and their community.
